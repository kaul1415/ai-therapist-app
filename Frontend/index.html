Welcome to MindBloom

Your personal AI-powered mental wellness companion — available 24/7, completely private, and always supportive.

What is MindBloom?

MindBloom is a mental wellness web app designed for students, young professionals, and anyone navigating stress, anxiety, or emotional overwhelm. It combines an AI chat companion, mood tracking, journaling, and wellness resources into one calm, private space.

Think of it as having a wise, supportive friend available at any hour — one who listens without judgment, helps you reflect, and gently guides you toward clarity.

MindBloom is not a replacement for professional therapy. If you're in crisis, please reach out to a licensed professional or crisis line. MindBloom is a tool for everyday emotional support and self-awareness.

Understanding Bloom's Chat Modes
🌊

Vent Mode

Bloom listens without advice or judgment. Use this when you just need to get it all out. No suggestions, no solutions — just a compassionate ear.

"I just need to talk…" → Bloom listens and reflects.
💡

Advice Mode

Bloom gently suggests coping strategies, techniques, and perspectives. Use this when you want practical help with what you're going through.

"I've been so anxious about exams" → Bloom offers strategies.
🔮

Clarity Mode

Bloom asks thoughtful questions to help you think through a situation yourself. Like a gentle coach helping you find your own answers.

"I don't know what to do…" → Bloom asks powerful questions.
Your Privacy
🔒

Your data stays with you

MindBloom stores all your personal data (journal entries, moods, chat history) only in your browser's local storage. This means it never leaves your device and is never sent to any server without your knowledge. Clearing your browser data will remove it — so consider exporting important entries.

AI conversations are sent to the backend for processing, but are not permanently stored on any server beyond what your backend is configured to keep.
